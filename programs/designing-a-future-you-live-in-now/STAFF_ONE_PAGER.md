# Staff One-Pager

## Designing a Future You Live In Now
A PRISM mentor-support framework for helping youth move from stuckness into present-day action.

## What it is
A structured way for staff to recognize why a young person is stuck, choose a useful intervention, and measure whether movement actually happens.

## Why it matters
Many youth are not blocked by a lack of desire alone. They are often blocked by one or more of the following:
- missing skill
- self-protection
- bad environment
- identity conflict
- lack of meaning
- confusion about what can and cannot be changed

## Core mentor job
Improve clarity, timing, environment, and next-step quality.

## Staff should be able to
- diagnose the likely barrier
- avoid shame-based responses
- distinguish skill gaps from self-protection
- separate gravity problems from workable problems
- use small activation strategies
- guide experiments instead of demanding certainty
- document evidence of movement

## The quick field sequence
1. Observe the current state
2. Classify the barrier
3. Choose the smallest useful intervention
4. Change environment if needed
5. Capture what happened
6. Set the next experiment

## Core tools
- stuckness diagnostics
- intervention heuristics
- environmental firewall
- authority and skepticism filters
- KPI and evaluation model

## Success looks like
- more self-efficacy
- fewer dysfunctional beliefs
- more prototype actions
- better decisions
- better mentor timing
- cleaner documentation and follow-through

## Bottom line
This framework helps staff stop guessing and start intervening with intention.
