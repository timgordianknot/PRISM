# KPIs and Evaluation

## Purpose
Measure whether the framework is increasing movement, confidence, and better mentor intervention quality.

## Participant KPIs
### Self-efficacy increase
Does the participant report greater belief that they can take useful action?

### Dysfunctional belief reduction
Are beliefs such as "I am too late" or "there is only one right path" weakening over time?

### Prototype frequency
How often is the participant trying real experiments such as conversations, classes, job shadows, or new routines?

### Decision quality
Is the participant making clearer decisions with less paralysis and less endless reconsideration?

### Environmental improvement
Have peers, inputs, routines, or space been improved in ways that support action?

## Staff KPIs
- diagnostic accuracy in case review
- intervention matching quality
- documentation completeness
- follow-through rate on next-step experiments
- ability to distinguish gravity problems from workable problems

## Evidence sources
- self-report check-ins
- mentor logs
- session reflections
- participation data
- prototype counts
- case review notes

## Review cadence
- weekly for mentor reflection
- biweekly for cohort review
- end-of-module for participant and staff learning summary

## Evaluation question
Are we helping people practice a better future now, or are we only helping them talk about one?
